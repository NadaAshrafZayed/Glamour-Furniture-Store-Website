Coastal Design

Coastal interior design draws inspiration from the natural beauty and relaxed atmosphere of coastal regions. It embraces elements of the seaside lifestyle, characterized by a palette of soft blues, sandy neutrals, and crisp whites, coastal interiors evoke the colors of the sea, sky, and sand. Furniture in coastal interiors is often casual and comfortable, with an emphasis on natural materials and relaxed silhouettes. Large windows and open floor plans maximize natural light and offer panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.
