Bohemian Design

The bohemian interior design style is all about being worldly and different. The boho style is perfect for those who want to express their individuality through their home decor. Bohemian decor includes bright colors, eclectic furniture, and a mix of patterns. Bohemian furniture often includes vintage pieces and antique accents. If you are looking for a design style that is truly unique, bohemian interior design is a great option for you!
